# 🌍 Global Stewardship: Foundational Frameworks

All documents are authored by Gaynor Kim-Sing using LLMs DeepSeek and Anthropic (Claude).

**THE QUESTION THESE DOCUMENTS ANSWER**

How do we build a future that reduces suffering — for all conscious beings — rather than amplifying it?

This repository's documents fall into **two tiers**, so readers can immediately find the kind of document they need. No folders to navigate — everything is listed below, grouped by tier.

| Tier | What it contains | How to read it |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1 — Governance & Evidence-Based Protocols** | Policy frameworks, legislative proposals, and protocols grounded in cited science, treaty language, and institutional mechanisms. Written for policymakers, diplomats, and institutions. | Cite, adopt, or submit these as-is in policy contexts. |
| **Tier 2 — Reflective & Contemplative Practice** | Personal and relational practices for inner integration, discernment, and coherence. Each carries a footer: *"Reflective Practice Document — not a GSC policy position."* | Use for personal practice, facilitation, or as background grounding for Tier 1 work — not as policy citation. |
